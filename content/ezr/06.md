## Ezra 06 Translation Questions ##

Q? What was found when King Darius ordered an investigation in the house of archives in Babylon?
A. During the investigation that King Darius ordered a scroll was found in the fortified city of Ecbatana in Media. [6:1-2]

Q? Who was to pay for the house for sacrifice mentioned in King Cyrus' decree?

A. The cost for the house of sacrifice was to be borne by the king's house. [6:4]

Q? According to King Cyrus' decree what was to be brought back to the house of God?

A. According to King Cyrus' decree the gold and silver objects belonging to the house of God that Nebuchadnezzar brought from the temple in Jerusalem to the temple in Babylon were to be brought back to the house of God in Jerusalem. [6:5]
 
Q? Who did Cyrus say would build the house of God?

A. Cyrus said the governor and Jewish elders would build the house of God. [6:7]

Q? Why did Cyrus want to give the Jews whatever they needed to build the house of God?

A. Cyrus wanted to give the Jews whatever they needed to build the house of God so they would bring the offering in to the God of Heaven and pray for him and his sons. [6:9-10]

Q? What must happen if anyone violates the decree about helping the Jews?

A. If anyone violates the decree, a beam must be pulled from his house and he must be impaled on it. His house must then be turned into a garbage heap because of this. [6:11]

Q? How did Haggai and Zechariah instruct the Jewish elders to build?

A. Haggai and Zechariah instructed the Jewish elders to build by prophesying. [6:14]

Q? For whom did the priests and Levites slaughter the Passover sacrifices?

A. The priests and Levites slaughtered the Passover sacrifices for all those who had been in exile, including themselves. [6:20]

Q? Why did the Jews joyfully celebrate the Festival of Unleavened Bread?

A. The Jews joyfully celebrated the Festival of Unleavened Bread, for Yahweh had brought them joy and turned the heart of Assyria's king to strengthen their hands in the work of his house. [6:22]
